# EGFR-Mutant NSCLC: Spotlight on Amivantamab.

## Abstract

Data from three phase III trials-PAPILLON, MARIPOSA, and MARIPOSA-2-highlight amivantamab, an EGFR-MET bispecific antibody, as a potential contender as a standard treatment for EGFR-mutant non-small cell lung cancer. Amivantamab paired with chemotherapy is a superior first-line option for patients with exon 20-mutated disease. Beyond this subset, compared with standard osimertinib, the combination with or without lazertinib also boosted progression-free survival, albeit at the cost of added toxicity.
