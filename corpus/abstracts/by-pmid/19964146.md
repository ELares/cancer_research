# Theoretical study for the treatment of pancreatic cancer using electric pulses.

## Abstract

Through the development of numerical models, this study describes how Non-Thermal Irreversible Electroporation (N-TIRE) of the pancreas presents certain challenges that can be alleviated through the use of non-puncturing plate electrodes and ultra-short electric pulses.
