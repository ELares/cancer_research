# A Dual-Armed Oncolytic Virus Shows Clinical Efficacy in Advanced Solid Cancers.

## Abstract

An oncolytic adenovirus armed with tumor necrosis factor-α and interleukin-2 was tested in patients with advanced solid tumors. Antitumor effects were observed in both treated and non-treated lesions, leading to long-term survival in some patients. This clinical trial shows the potential of oncolytic virotherapy for patients refractory to standard therapies. See related article by Pakola et al., p. 3715.
