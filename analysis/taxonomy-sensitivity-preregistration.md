# Taxonomy Sensitivity Pre-Registration

Pre-registered BEFORE running any alternative groupings.
Commit this document before implementing or running sensitivity analysis code.

## Current taxonomy

19 mechanisms, including 7 physical/energy categories:
TTFields (262 articles), HIFU (81), sonodynamic (187), electrochemical-therapy (185),
frequency-therapy (71), electrolysis (11), bioelectric (182).

The manuscript (Section 3.6) acknowledges: "the 19-mechanism taxonomy has not been tested
for sensitivity: collapsing the 7 physical/energy categories into 2-3 broader groups
would reduce the number of 'zero publication gaps'."

## Robustness targets

### Must survive (core claims)
1. Immunotherapy is the most-published mechanism under any grouping
2. The nanoparticle maturity gap (high volume, low clinical evidence) persists
3. At least one physical modality shows Phase III clinical evidence (TTFields)

### May be taxonomy-dependent (acknowledged)
4. The specific count of zero-publication gaps (currently 94 cells in the 19×22 matrix)
5. Physical mechanisms dominate the gap list
6. The number of "preclinical-stuck" mechanisms

## Stability definition

- **Stable**: conclusion holds under ALL 3 alternative groupings plus the baseline
- **Taxonomy-dependent**: conclusion changes under at least one grouping
